About

I am an independent consultant specializing in commercial analytics and forecasting for pharmaceutical and life sciences companies. With over 20 years of experience — including the past 7 as an independent advisor — I help teams make confident, data-driven decisions from pre-launch through in-market optimization.

Clients such as Amgen, Kite, AbbVie, Genentech, Lilly, and Sanofi rely on me for rigorous analytics, clear insights, and fit-for-purpose solutions. My work spans oncology, rare diseases, and specialty therapeutics, often addressing high-stakes questions with significant business impact.

I’m known for quickly stepping in, integrating seamlessly with internal teams, and delivering high-quality work without the learning curve. Whether leading a complex forecast, designing market research, or synthesizing diverse data sources, I bring a collaborative, results-focused approach that helps clients move forward with clarity and confidence.
